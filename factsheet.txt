PRODUCT OVERVIEW
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

VisionAI Copilot v3.0 is an enterprise-grade AI assistant integrated directly into your productivity suite. It automates repetitive knowledge tasks, drafts communications, analyzes reports, writes code, and answers complex questions grounded in your organization's private document library.

VisionAI Copilot is powered by TechVision's proprietary large language model fine-tuned on enterprise workflows, combined with a real-time Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) backend.

KEY FEATURES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. DOCUMENT Q&A
   Ask any question across your entire enterprise document library — contracts,
   SOPs, HR policies, financial reports, technical manuals. Answers are always
   cited back to source documents with page-level precision.

2. MEETING INTELLIGENCE
   Connect to your calendar. VisionAI Copilot transcribes meetings in real-time,
   generates action items, and drafts follow-up emails automatically.

3. CODE GENERATION & REVIEW
   Supports 40+ programming languages. Generates boilerplate, refactors
   spaghetti code, and flags potential security vulnerabilities inline.

4. AUTOMATED REPORT DRAFTING
   Provide raw data (CSV, JSON, Excel). VisionAI generates executive summaries,
   visualizations, and slide decks ready for board presentations.

5. MULTI-MODAL ANALYSIS
   Analyze charts, diagrams, photographs, and scanned documents. Extract
   structured data from unstructured visual content.
